# Example: Fashion Brand Logo

## Brand Brief

**Brand**: Aether
**Industry**: Sustainable Luxury Fashion
**Values**: Elegance, Sustainability, Timelessness, Conscious Luxury
**Style**: Luxury, Minimalist, Timeless
**Colors**: Black, white, gold accents

## Generated Concepts

### Concept 1: The Thread

**Style**: Minimalist / Luxury

**Visual Description**:
A single continuous thread forming the letter "A" with elegant curves. The line weight varies subtly, like a calligraphy stroke.

**SVG Code**:
```svg

```

**Rationale**:
- Single thread = minimal waste, sustainability
- Clean lines = luxury and sophistication
- Gold dot = premium quality mark
- Works on fabric tags, packaging, digital

### Concept 2: Interlocking A

**Style**: Lettermark / Geometric

**Visual Description**:
Two interlocking shapes forming an abstract "A". Inspired by fabric folds and sustainable loop concept.

**Rationale**:
- Interlocking = connection, community
- Loop shape = sustainability, circular fashion
- Geometric = modern luxury
- Highly versatile mark

### Concept 3: Horizon Line

**Style**: Abstract / Conceptual

**Visual Description**:
A horizon line that curves upward, suggesting elevation and aspiration. Represents the "aether" - the upper air, the refined.

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #0A0A0A (Deep Black)
- Secondary: #FFFFFF (Pure White)
- Accent: #BFA15F (Soft Gold)

## Brand Guidelines Excerpt

**Minimum Size**: 20mm width for print

**Clear Space**: Equal to the height of the letter "A"

**Color Applications**:
- Primary: Black on white
- Secondary: White on black
- Special: Gold foil on black packaging

**Typography Pairing**:
- Logo: Custom mark
- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond
- Body: Futura Light

The logo successfully launched across:
- Flagship store signage
- Clothing labels and hang tags
- Packaging boxes and bags
- Digital platform
- Print campaigns

All while maintaining the brand's commitment to sustainability through minimal ink usage and timeless design.
