# Example: Coffee Shop Logo

## Brand Brief

**Brand**: Slow Brew
**Industry**: Specialty Coffee / Cafe
**Values**: Craftsmanship, Patience, Quality, Community
**Style**: Vintage, Handcrafted, Warm
**Colors**: Earth tones, warm browns, cream

## Generated Concepts

### Concept 1: The Pour

**Style**: Minimalist Illustration

**Visual Description**:
A graceful coffee pour creating a spiral shape. The stream of coffee forms an abstract "S". Clean line art with slight hand-drawn feel.

**SVG Code**:
```svg
<svg viewBox="0 0 100 100" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
  <path d="M30 20 Q50 10 70 20 Q85 30 70 40 Q50 50 30 40 Q15 30 30 20"
        fill="#8B4513"/>
  <path d="M50 35 Q60 50 50 80 Q40 50 50 35"
        fill="#D2691E"/>
  <ellipse cx="50" cy="25" rx="15" ry="5" fill="#654321"/>
</svg>
```

**Rationale**:
- Captures the slow pour moment
- S shape = Slow Brew
- Warm colors evoke coffee and comfort
- Simple enough for stamp, packaging, signage

### Concept 2: Vintage Badge

**Style**: Emblem / Badge

**Visual Description**:
Circular badge with "SLOW BREW" arching over a central coffee cup illustration. Established date banner below. Vintage typography.

**Rationale**:
- Communicates heritage and craft
- Badge format = trust and tradition
- Works on signage, merchandise
- Strong brand presence

### Concept 3: Bean & Leaf

**Style**: Organic / Natural

**Visual Description**:
A coffee bean split to reveal a growing sprout inside. Represents fresh roasting and growth.

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #5D4037 (Dark Roast)
- Secondary: #8D6E63 (Medium)
- Accent: #D7CCC8 (Cream)

## Final Application

The Pour concept was selected and applied to:
- Coffee cups (printed)
- Store signage (backlit)
- Packaging bags
- Loyalty cards
- Social media avatar

All work beautifully at different sizes while maintaining the warm, crafted feel.
