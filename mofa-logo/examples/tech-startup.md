# Example: Tech Startup Logo

## Brand Brief

**Brand**: ByteFlow
**Industry**: AI Infrastructure / Developer Tools
**Values**: Innovation, Speed, Reliability, Developer-friendly
**Style**: Modern, Minimalist, Abstract
**Colors**: Blue trust, but open to suggestions

## Generated Concepts

### Concept 1: Data Stream

**Style**: Abstract + Minimalist

**Visual Description**:
A continuous flowing line forming the letter "B" in motion. The line starts thick and tapers, suggesting data flow and transformation.

**SVG Code**:
```svg
<svg viewBox="0 0 100 100" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
  <path d="M20 35 Q50 15 80 35 T80 65 Q50 85 20 65"
        fill="none" stroke="#2563EB" stroke-width="8" stroke-linecap="round"/>
  <path d="M20 50 Q50 30 80 50"
        fill="none" stroke="#60A5FA" stroke-width="6" stroke-linecap="round"/>
</svg>
```

**Rationale**:
- Flowing lines represent data pipelines
- Abstract B letter provides brand recognition
- Two overlapping strokes suggest data transformation layers
- Clean enough for favicon, impactful for billboard

### Concept 2: Node Network

**Style**: Geometric + Abstract

**Visual Description**:
Three interconnected nodes forming a triangular shape, with one node highlighted. Represents distributed systems and AI processing.

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #0EA5E9 (Sky Blue)
- Secondary: #64748B (Slate)
- Accent: #06B6D4 (Cyan)

### Concept 3: Binary Leaf

**Style**: Organic + Tech fusion

**Visual Description**:
A stylized leaf made of circuit-like lines, combining nature (growth) with technology (binary/digital). The stem forms a subtle "B".

**Rationale**:
- Represents growth and scalability
- Circuit patterns = technology
- Organic form = human-friendly AI
- Unique fusion of natural and digital

## Final Selection

**Chosen**: Concept 1 (Data Stream)

**Reason**: Best balance of tech feel and simplicity. Works at all sizes. Most distinctive among competitors.

## Variations Created

1. **Monochrome**: Single navy blue line
2. **Reversed**: White lines on dark blue background
3. **Icon only**: Just the B mark, no text
4. **Horizontal layout**: Mark + wordmark side by side
